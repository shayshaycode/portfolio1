Professional Summary

As a passionate and experienced educator with a strong background in business management and psychology, I have spent the last several years designing and delivering engaging, student-centered curricula in a diverse, high-performance international school setting. My expertise in lesson planning, communication, and learning engagement has given me a solid foundation in understanding how learners absorb, process, and apply new information.

Now, after completing a specialized certification in Instructional Design from Emiritus, I am transitioning into the field of e-learning and corporate training. Throughout this program, I have gained hands-on experience with leading authoring tools like Articulate Storyline, Adobe Captivate, and Learning Management Systems (LMS), enabling me to design interactive and effective learning experiences for diverse audiences.
